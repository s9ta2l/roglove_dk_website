We are a startup who are aiming to make hand rehabilitation fun!

We are developing an electronic glove in combination with an app. Allowing people and patients to play fun retro games while completing their rehabilitative and preventative hand physiotherapy exercises. Our glove will be able to record hand mobility data and monitor the progress, giving the user personalized feedback and recommended treatment or preventative hand care plans. Our product is for anyone, whether they are athletes - serious about their hands, or patients struggling to motivate themselves to complete their at-home physiotherapy exercises.
